About me

In brief

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area and split my time between San Francisco, Palo Alto, and Walnut Creek.

I'm an incoming graduate student at Stanford University where I plan to study healthcare and technology as a fully-funded Knight-Hennessy Scholar. Most recently, I was a consultant at Boston Consulting Group, where I've also designed community health centers, government agencies, medical practices, schools, and surgery centers. Before that, I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2023 with degrees in Data Science and Business Administration.

Please don't hesitate to reach out at danielzhuwork@gmail.com or danzhulife@gmail.com.

More about me

My name is Daniel Zhu, but plenty of people call me Dan. I'm 25 and my birthday is in January.

My current home is San Francisco and the broader Bay Area; I've recently lived in Walnut Creek, Lafayette, Berkeley, and some of San Francisco's greatest neighborhoods (North Beach, The Mission, Russian Hill). During other periods of my life, I've also lived in Los Angeles, Fairfield (CA), Davis (CA), and London (UK).

I've spent the past decade studying how organizations and technologies impact society and I'm grateful for the many opportunities I've had to apply my learnings, particularly in the world of healthcare. While at UC Berkeley, I applied my Data Science and Business Administration degrees to address problems in education, healthcare, and housing. While at BCG, I designed new approaches to behavioral health, care management, and value-based care that have collectively impacted more than 35 million Americans. (more on my work here)

This September, I'm starting a new chapter at Stanford University as a fully-funded Knight Hennessy Scholar. Formally, I'll be getting an MBA through the Stanford Graduate School of Business and an MS in Community Health through the Stanford School of Medicine, but I'm equally looking forward to engaging with talented peers on society's most important challenges.

In addition to school and work, I aim to both consume and create. I track the food I eat and the books I read, but I'm also trying to cook and write more! I volunteer with a wide variety of organizations but the one I care about most is Asian Health Services. I also enjoy learning about our planet through long walks/hikes and the people that call it home through deep conversations and dinner parties. (more on my life here)
